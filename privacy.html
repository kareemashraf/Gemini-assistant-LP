Privacy Policy for Gemini™ AI Assistant Chrome Extension.

Last updated: November 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gemini™ AI Assistant. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our browser extension. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from our users. Our extension operates locally in your browser and does not transmit any personal data to our servers or third parties. Specifically:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect your name, email address, IP address, or any other information that could be used to identify you.
  • Local Processing: All AI processing and analysis of web page content are performed directly within your browser. No content from the web pages you visit is sent to our servers for processing.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your browsing history, usage patterns, or interactions with the extension.
  • Local Storage: We use local storage (e.g., browser's localStorage) solely to save your preference for dark/light mode, which remains on your device.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use it for any purpose.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose any information with third parties because we do not collect any data from our users.

5. Security

While we do not collect or store your personal data, we are committed to ensuring that your experience with our extension is secure. Our extension is designed with privacy and security in mind, ensuring all sensitive operations occur client-side.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
